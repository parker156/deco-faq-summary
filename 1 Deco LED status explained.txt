LED status on Deco explained as follows, 
LED off meaning not powered on
Solid yellow meaning booting
Solid red meaning no Internet on Main Deco
Flashing blue means that this unit is ready to be setup
Solid green means that the Deco is working properly without issues
For satellite Deco units, flashing red means that the satellite is disconnected from the main Deco, Main Deco does not have a flashing red state.
